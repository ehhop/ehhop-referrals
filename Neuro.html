If a curbside is sufficient, coordinate with the TS to have Dr. Fabian see the patient.

Cost

The cost of a curbside is free. The cost of an appointment for patients with charity care level 1 is $15.

Wait time

It takes about 6 weeks after a patient has received charity care for a neurology appointment to be scheduled. Urgent referrals can be expedited. Contact Kristen Ng if the referral is urgent and needs to be expedited.
