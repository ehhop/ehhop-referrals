Please follow the Instructions below to upload documents on Epic:

  1. Click the "More Activities" button on the bottom left of the patient's chart on Epic
  2. Click "Media Manager"
  3. On the new window that opens up, click "Import"
  4. Find the file you would like to upload. You will need to find the file manually (i.e. go to "Computer", then "C:", then to the folder where you stored the file when you uploaded it). The Documents/Pictures/Music folders on the left do not correspond to the same folders on your workstation.
  5. Enter the name of the document, the type of document it is, and who you would like to get a notification that the document has been uploaded (usually the clinic team).
  6. Click the accept button at the very bottom of the screen. You might need to resize your windows a little bit go find it.
