X-Rays are walk-in and free for EHHOP patients.

Senior Clinician

  • Please ask your patient to go to the 6th floor of 17 East 102nd Street any time between 9 am and 4:30pm M-F.
  • During sign-out, let the head TS know that your patient needs an X-Ray and why the referral is necessary.
  • No additional paperwork is required.

    • Teaching Senior

  • Please place an order for the referral on Epic.
  • Please include the referral in the email to referrals@mssm.edu after clinic.

    • Access to Care Team

  • No need to see patient unless the clinic team requests it.
  • If the clinic team requests you see the patient, please ask them to go to the 6th floor of 17 East 102nd Street any time between 9am and 5pm M-F.

    • Cost

    Cost to EHHOP Patient: Free

    Cost to society:

  • Chest X-ray ~$370
  • Knee X-Ray ~$200
  • Skull X-Ray ~$290
  • Abdominal X-Ray ~$240
  • X-Ray Spine ~$280
  • Shoulder X-Ray ~$210
  • X-Ray Rib ~$220
  • Hip X-Ray ~$260
  • Foot X-Ray ~$280

    • Wait time

    Appointments are walk-in. Patients can receive their X-Rays whenever they go to 6th floor CAM during the weekday.

    Hours of service: 9am-4:30pm M-F
