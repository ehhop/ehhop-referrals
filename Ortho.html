    Cost

    Cost to EHHOP Patient: Once your patient submits documents confirming their income, Financial Services will grant you patient Charity Care and place them on a sliding scale. Most EHHOP patients are placed on Level 1. For patients with Level 1, outpatient appointments will cost $15 and procedures will be free. To find out the costs for patients who are not Level 1, please contact the Emerging Referrals Manager, Kristen Ng

    Cost to Society: Outpatient Orthopedic appointments normally cost $180 out of pocket in New York.

    Wait time

    How long it takes patients to complete their appointment will depend on how soon the patients can submit all their documents confirming their income to Financial Services. This process takes, on average, 20 days. Once all documents are submitted, it takes about 10 weeks for an appointment with Orthopedics.
