    Once your patient has charity care, please contact the MRI Referrals Manager. The MRI referrals manager will give you directions on how to schedule your patient for their specialty referral appointment.

    Cost

    Cost to EHHOP Patient: Once your patient submits documents confirming their income, Financial Services will grant you patient Charity Care and place them on a sliding scale. Most EHHOP patients are placed on Level 1. For patients with Level 1, the cost of the MRI will vary between $15 and $125 depending on the body part and whether contrast is used or not. An abdominal MRI w and w/o contrast will cost $101.6. And MRI w/o contrast will cost $15. To find out the costs for other body parts, and for patients who are not Level 1, please contact the MRI rep, Alex Rosen.

    Wait time

    How long it takes patients to complete their appointment will depend on how soon the patients can submit all their documents confirming their income to Financial Services (which takes ~24 days on averge). Once all documents are submitted, it takes about 1 week for an MRI.

    Pre-scan requirements

    Radiology Associates will need to know the following information:

    • Whether the patient has liver or kidney disease.
    • A BMP withing six weeks of the scan must be on file at the time of scan
    • Does the patient have any metal in their body (e.g. pacemaker)?
    • Is the patient allergic to contrast?
