Cost

To obtain hearing aids, we use a program called Hear Now. The cost to the patient is the application fee, which is $125 per hearing aid. Therefore, if the patient needs to hearing aids, they will cost $250.

Wait time

Since there are many steps in this referral, required from various stakeholders, wait time will vary. It can be anywhere from a couple weeks to multiple months.
